About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

The Plaza Hotel, designed by Edward Kingsman, opened on 15 May 1925 and gave the station an classic oldstyle english touch. It had 150 bedrooms spread over seven floors. Over the years our hotel hosted people from all over the world, and introduced them to the beauties of english architecture. In 1986 the booking hall and ticket offices were modernised and next year in 1987 the whole hotel was remodelized. Today it has 300 rooms spread over twenty floors, and an unique classic and modern style combined together, wich has rightfully earned our hotel many international awards.
